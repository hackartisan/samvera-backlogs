Resolves HELIO-2495:'blank' in ISBN field causes Monograph to come up…
… in searches erroneously <fieldType class="solr.TextField" name="isbn" positionIncrementGap="100">   <analyzer>     <tokenizer class="solr.KeywordTokenizerFactory"/>     <filter class="solr.PatternReplaceTokenFilterFactory" pattern="[^\dXx]" replacement=""/>     <filter class="solr.LengthFilterFactory" min="10" max="13"/>     <filter class="solr.LowerCaseFilterFactory"/>   </analyzer> </fieldType>  The PatternReplaceTokenFilterFactory was return "" for 'blank' which was valid, so I add the LengthFilterFactory because valid isbns are 10 to 13 digits. Now it work better!
