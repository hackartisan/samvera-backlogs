Adding ReCAPTCHA to email form to stop spam
Uses Google's ReCAPTCHA service to stop spam from getting through via our contact form. Configuration of the service is documented here: http://sites.psu.edu/dltdocs/?p=4084 And the server configs have been updated, as noted in: http://sites.psu.edu/dltdocs/?p=3521 This has been tested in QA, but still requires some UI work and accessibility verification.
