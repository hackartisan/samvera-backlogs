Feature: Reporting and Analytics
MVP components:  Google analytics integration Library Staff dashboard reporting, including:  Individual collections: number of objects and total file size), Individual objects: number of pageviews Individual files: number of downloads and pageviews   Administrator-level reporting, including user activity, date-range selections, repository-wide statistics (total objects/collections/files; most common file types; growth in past 90 days)  This functionality will require monitoring Hyrax community activity and will have close dependencies with our front-end Discovery application.
