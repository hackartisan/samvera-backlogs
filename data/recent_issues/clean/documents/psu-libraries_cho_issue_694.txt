Expand use of ARKs
There are many other potential uses of ARKs at University Libraries. They may or may not be directly related to CHO. Documenting there in the backlog as a marker.  descriptive records in the library catalog descriptive records in archives catalog (Aspace -- staff or public) OR Finding Aid authority records preservation object (AIP in preservation management system) [x] access object (CHO)  University of Houston has ARKs for all of these. They use NOID to run an ARK server to resolve all of these locally, not using the N2T resolver. Some are not actionable (AIP).
