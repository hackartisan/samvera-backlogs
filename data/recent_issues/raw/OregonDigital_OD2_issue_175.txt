POST a marker to honeycomb during production/staging deployment
Descriptive summary POST a marker to honeycomb that a deployment happened: $ curl https://api.honeycomb.io/1/markers/myservice -X POST  -H "X-Honeycomb-Team: ${HONEYCOMB_KEY}"  -d '{"message":"${RAILS_ENV} - ${DEPLOYED_VERSION} deployed", "type":"deploy"}'
