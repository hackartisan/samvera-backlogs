Provide error logging when files cannot be successfully attached by importer
When files have errors (corrupt, virus, etc.) that prevent them from being attached, ensure that the importer logs the issue for example see the following works https://ursus.library.ucla.edu/catalog/wd375w70x https://ursus.library.ucla.edu/catalog/6395w749d https://ursus.library.ucla.edu/catalog/kp78gg666
