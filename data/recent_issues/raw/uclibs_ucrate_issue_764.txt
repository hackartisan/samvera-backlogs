Creating DOI on existing work doesn't auto-fill the Publisher field
Descriptive summary When editing an existing work and choosing to mint a new DOI, the publisher field is not being automatically populated with "University of Cincinnati." (It does work properly when creating a new work and new DOI at the same time) Expected behavior If the Publisher field is blank, it gets automatically populated with "University of Cincinnati" when the user chooses to create a DOI Actual behavior The Publisher field remains blank Steps to reproduce the behavior  Create a work without a DOI and with a blank publisher Save the work Edit the work and choose to mint a new DOI Save the work You will be returned to the edit form complaining that the Publisher field cannot be blank.
