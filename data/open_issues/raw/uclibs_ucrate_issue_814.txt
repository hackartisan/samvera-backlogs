DOI Publisher form validation
Descriptive summary We want validation on the publisher field contingent on DOI minting status. If a user requests a DOI, the Publisher field must not be blank. There's likely two possible routes to acheive this validation:  Tie into the JS status bar/validation on the right column of the submission form to prevent  submission contingent on publisher/DOI presence. Model validation after submission  We currently have a model validation that results in a runtime error: resubmitting the form after failed validation results in Unknown agent type "" error.
