Assign works to collections based on academic unit
Descriptive summary When a work is assigned an academic unit, the work should be automatically assigned to the related collection. For example, when the user selects Department of Chemistry on the form, the work should be placed in the Department of Chemistry collection. Related work Sorta related: #1605
If a work is edited, and the academic unit is altered, on Save the item should be removed from the old collection and placed in the new collection. FOR EXAMPLE: my work was originally in Department of Chemistry Academic Unit and thus in the Department of Chemistry Collection, but i edit the work to change the Academic Unit to Animal Sciences. The work should be removed from Department of Chemistry Collection and added to Department of Animal Sciences Collection.
